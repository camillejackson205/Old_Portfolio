I took this interest and ran with it, especially with the idea in the back of my mind that technology is where the world is going. After high school is when the world really became my canvas and I traveled within the states for 2 years. During these two years of hiking in national forests, swimming in waterfalls, meeting beautiful new people, I continuously imerced myself into code and became more and more interested in technology over the duration of time. With my gracious love for nature and exploring, this industry reminds me that I can have both which is something I find extremely giving. Though life is not always seamless, there will always be bugs to fix, improvements to be made and coffee to grind. After all, life really teaches you that the obstacle is the path.
